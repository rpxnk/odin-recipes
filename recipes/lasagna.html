World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic Italian dish that features layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous topping of melted cheese. It's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with chopped onion and minced garlic until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet with the meat mixture; simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by half of the ricotta mixture, a third of the mozzarella cheese, and a third of the meat sauce.
  8. Repeat layers, ending with noodles topped with remaining meat sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
