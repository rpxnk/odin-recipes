Strawberry Lemonade Poke Cake

Description

This Strawberry Lemonade Poke Cake is a refreshing dessert perfect for summer gatherings. The cake is infused with lemonade and topped with a luscious strawberry glaze, making it a delightful treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 box lemon cake mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup lemonade concentrate, thawed
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the lemon cake mix, water, vegetable oil, and eggs. Mix until well combined.
  3. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  4. While the cake is baking, prepare the lemonade glaze by mixing the lemonade concentrate with powdered sugar and milk until smooth.
  5. Once the cake is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for about 10 minutes. Poke holes all over the top of the cake using a fork or skewer.
  6. Pour the lemonade glaze evenly over the warm cake, allowing it to soak in.
  7. Let the cake cool completely, then top with sliced strawberries and whipped cream if desired.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow flavors to meld.
